About Our School

Our Rich History

Founded on the principles of excellence and discipline, Kibabii Highschool has grown froma humble beginning into anational academic powerhouse in Bungoma county. For Decades, we have been the forefront of molding young boys into responsible citizens and leaders of tommorrow.

Our school is known for its strong culture of hardwork, which is reflected in our consistent perfomance in the KCSE National Examination and our dominance in regional sports and drama festivals.

At Kibabii Highschool, we don't just teach students; we inspire them to dream big. Our holistic approach to education ensure that every student who passes through our gates leaves with the character and skills needed to serve the nation. Welcome to our community of excellence.
